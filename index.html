JOIN US Mission

The Public Policy and Opinion Cell (PPOC) at IIT Kanpur is a student-run initiative dedicated
to fostering informed discussions on global and national policy issues. As the only
public policy cell among the 23 IITs in India, PPOC serves as a platform for students to
engage with complex topics spanning politics, economics, history, and governance
